## Featured Photo Series

Dionne Bunsha's photography focuses on social and environmental documentation, particularly covering agricultural crises, regional development, and landscapes across India.

Featured photo series include:
- Beej Bachao (seed conservation)
- Bella Bella (landscape/nature series)
- Clayoquot (environmental documentation)
- Fisherman portraits
- Gangotri (Himalayan region)
- Himalaya series
- Gujarat Farmers Suicide documentation
- Sabarkantha region
- Sugarcane agricultural work
- Vidarbha region

## Photography Practice

Her photographic work accompanies her investigative journalism, providing visual documentation of environmental degradation, agricultural crises, indigenous communities, and human rights issues. The photographs serve as primary source material for her reporting and bear witness to the conditions and communities she documents.

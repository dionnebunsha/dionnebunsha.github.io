## About Dionne Bunsha

Dionne Bunsha is a journalist and photographer who has written extensively on topics including:
- Gujarat violence and its aftermath
- Human rights
- Climate change and environment
- Farm crisis
- Globalization
- Mumbai-based reporting

## Notable Work

Bunsha authored a book titled "Scarred: Experiments with Violence in Gujarat," which received acclaim from acclaimed author Arundhati Roy, who stated the work "reveals the awful routine business of everyday fascism."
